Couples Communication Study


Participate in a paid study oncommunication in romantic relationships. Participation in this study involves you and your partner coming into the NYU Couples Lab together to respond to some questionnaires and recorded conversations. Participation takes approximately an hour.

Couples Cognitive Performance Study

Participate in a paid study on cognitive performance in the presence of romantic partners. Participation in this study involves you and your partner coming into the NYU Couples Lab together to respond to some questionnaires and complete a few challenging verbal, spatial, perceptual and computational tasks. Participation takes approximately half an hour.

Witnessing Conflict Study


This study explores what perceptions people hold of various roles in society. Participants will be asked to complete questionnaires and watch a short video.
