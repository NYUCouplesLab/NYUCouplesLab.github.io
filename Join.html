Research Assistant Opportunities

As a RA, you have the opportunity to be involved in all stages of studying close relationships including subject recruitment, instrument development, data collection, analysis and interpretation, and preparation for publications. Undergraduates can visit the Undergraduate Course Catalog and Masters student can visit the MA Course Catalog for more information.

Graduate Student Opportunities

Graduate students join the New York University Couples Lab via the Doctoral Program in Psychology, and may seek admission to the program through the Social area of specialization.

Postdoctoral Fellows and Visiting Scholars

If interested in working in the lab as a post-doc or visiting scholar, please contact our principal investigator Dr. Patrick Shrout.
